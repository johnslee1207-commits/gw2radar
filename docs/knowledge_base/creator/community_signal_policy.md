Creator Intelligence should use community discussion as discovery input, not as strong fact.

Policy notes:

- store summaries and source links only;
- do not store copied full text;
- preserve attribution;
- treat community claims as low-confidence until reviewed;
- do not ingest private community spaces without explicit authorization.
