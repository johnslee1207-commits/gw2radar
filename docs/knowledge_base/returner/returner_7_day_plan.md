A returner recovery plan should reduce cognitive load before optimizing long-term goals.

Suggested explanation structure:

- confirm what the account already owns;
- recover basic map movement and unlock awareness;
- choose one low-friction build or open-world setup;
- pick one goal for the next seven days;
- postpone expensive or confusing goals until readiness improves.
