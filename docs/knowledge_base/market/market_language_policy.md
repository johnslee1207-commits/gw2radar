Market explanations should support planning, manual review, and goal-cost awareness.

Policy notes:

- avoid guaranteed-profit language;
- do not recommend automated orders;
- do not support manipulation or real-money exchange;
- frame buy-wait and hold signals as manual planning guidance;
- keep goal materials protected before surplus decisions.
